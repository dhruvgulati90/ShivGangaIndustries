Black HIPS Granule Ranges
Grade 1 ₹48/kg
Application: Photo Frames & Handicraft Items This economical grade is ideal for small-scale molding and decorative applications. It offers good finish and workability, making it a popular choice for photo frame manufacturers and handicraft industries.
Grade 2 ₹52/kg
Application: Globe Manufacturing Specially formulated for globe and educational product manufacturers, this grade provides better shine and durability, perfect for round molding and precision detailing.
Grade 3 ₹60/kg
Application: LED Televisions (Up to 32 Inches) This mid-range grade is engineered for LED TV back covers up to 32 inches. It balances impact strength, surface finish, and flow properties, making it ideal for mass production.
Grade 4 ₹70/kg
Application: Televisions (40 Inches and Above) Our premium-grade granules, designed for large-size LED TVs (40” and above). With superior toughness and heat resistance, this grade ensures durability and high-end aesthetics for larger electronic housings.
